It needs the right training by the right persons, experience of making mistakes and learning from them and working together with the right motivation. And that is what you will find in DSi.

People who know what to test and what not to test, people who have honed the skill of analyzing a problem and coming up with a solution step by step, in iterations, until they have the best solution. And that is the kind of people you will find in DSi.
